DS Marriage Lawn is a beautiful and grand place designed especially for village-side weddings and family celebrations. With a large green lawn, peaceful surroundings, and clean, attractive decoration, it gives every function a warm and special feeling. Our place is known for its bright lighting, strong stage setup, and comfortable seating that makes every guest feel welcomed and respected.
